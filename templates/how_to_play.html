How to Play
Here is a quick guide on how to use SENTI.

Step 1. Click the play button on the home page.
Step 2. Enter your text in the text box and then click the start button.
Step 3. SENTI analyzes the text and tells you the sentiment/mood of the text.
Step 4. If your satisfied with the output, press YES, otherwise press NO , providing us with valuable feedback for further improving our Model.
